## Notes for GM

**Haggling:** Players can attempt Diplomacy checks to reduce prices by 10% (DC 15) or 20% (DC 20).

**Special Orders:** Merchants can order items not in stock, arriving in 1d4 days for common items, 1d6+2 days for uncommon, 2d6+4 days for rare.

**Selling:** Merchants buy items at 50% of listed price (common), 40% (uncommon), 30% (rare).

**Reputation:** If players have helped the town, merchants may offer 10% discount on all purchases.
