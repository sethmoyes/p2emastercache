*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This nervous gnome sailor lost their parents at a young age. In their youth, they had an unremarkable childhood. They knew someone in the criminal underworld who greatly influenced them. At one point, they publicly embarrassed someone, motivated by family. Now they are traveling to Otari for work.*

**Proprietor:** Una Thornblade (Gnome Sailor)

**Specialties:** General goods and supplies
