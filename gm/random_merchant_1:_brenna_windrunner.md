*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This friendly dwarf hunter was born to privilege among the nobility. Their parents were yeomen. In their youth, they took a life at a young age. They had a significant romantic relationship who greatly influenced them. At one point, they caused suffering through inaction, motivated by hatred. Now they are visiting family in Otari.*

**Proprietor:** Brenna Windrunner (Dwarf Hunter)

**Specialties:** Tools and equipment
