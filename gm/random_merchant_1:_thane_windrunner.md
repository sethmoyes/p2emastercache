*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This quiet elf priest was born to privilege among the nobility. Their parents were tradespeople. In their youth, they survived a raid on their settlement. They was influenced by a holy person who greatly influenced them. At one point, they failed to keep an important oath, motivated by jealousy. Now they are visiting family in Otari.*

**Proprietor:** Thane Windrunner (Elf Priest)
