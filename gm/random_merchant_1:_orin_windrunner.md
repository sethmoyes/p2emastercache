*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This secretive half-orc innkeeper born to merchants or artisans. Their parents were tradespeople. In their youth, they ran with a rough crowd. They had a wise teacher who greatly influenced them. At one point, they failed to keep an important oath, motivated by religion. Now they are traveling to Otari for work.*

**Proprietor:** Orin Windrunner (Half-Orc Innkeeper)
