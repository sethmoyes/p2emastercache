*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This quiet halfling soldier was born to privilege among the nobility. Their parents were soldiers. In their youth, they was kidnapped and later escaped. They had a wise teacher who greatly influenced them. At one point, they publicly embarrassed someone, motivated by religion. Now they are escorting goods to market.*

**Proprietor:** Milo Emberforge (Halfling Soldier)

**Specialties:** Books and scrolls
