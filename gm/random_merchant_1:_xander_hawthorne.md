# Random Merchant 1: Xander Hawthorne

*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This confident human craftsperson raised by another family. Their parents were merchants. In their youth, they was kidnapped and later escaped. They was close to a family member who greatly influenced them. At one point, they lied to further their goals, motivated by pressured. Now they are seeking adventure.*

**Proprietor:** Xander Hawthorne (Human Craftsperson)

**Specialties:** Weapons and armor
