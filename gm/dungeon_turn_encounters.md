# Dungeon Turn Encounters - Gauntlight Keep

**Party Level:** 4
**Floors Covered:** 1-10
**Generated:** 960 encounters (sums 5-100 for each floor)

---

## How to Use

1. When the dice jar reaches 5 dice, roll all 5d20
2. Sum the total
3. Find your current floor level below
4. Look up the sum in that floor's table
5. Read the encounter to your players

## Probability Distribution

- **5-35:** Beneficial/Very Minor (~10%)
- **36-52:** Flavor Events - Low (~20%)
- **53-69:** Flavor Events - High (~20%)
- **70-100:** Hazards/Encounters (~10%)

The middle 50% (36-69) are flavor events that teach about the world.
