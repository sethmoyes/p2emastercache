# Random Merchant 2: Xander Proudfoot

<div align="center">

<img src="https://pathfinderwiki.com/w/images/0/05/Xander.jpg" alt="Proprietor" width="250">

</div>

*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This secretive elf scribe born to merchants or artisans. Their parents were tradespeople. In their youth, they attended a private academy. They was influenced by a holy person who greatly influenced them. At one point, they failed to keep an important oath, motivated by justice. Now they are searching for a lost relative.*

**Proprietor:** Xander Proudfoot (Elf Scribe)

**Specialties:** Tools and equipment
