*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This cowardly human priest grew up among peasants, working the land. Their parents were yeomen. In their youth, they was betrayed by a trusted friend or family member. They knew an athletic or competitive person who greatly influenced them. At one point, they lied to further their goals, motivated by hatred. Now they are fleeing from trouble.*

**Proprietor:** Orin Youngblood (Human Priest)

**Specialties:** Alchemical items and potions
