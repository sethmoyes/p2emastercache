# Random Merchant 2: Sable Windrunner

<div align="center">

<img src="https://2e.aonprd.com/Images/Monsters/Merchant.webp" alt="Proprietor" width="250">

*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This friendly halfling innkeeper grew up among peasants, working the land. Their parents were yeomen. In their youth, they grew up during wartime. They had a wise teacher who greatly influenced them. At one point, they caused suffering through inaction, motivated by pressured. Now they are running from the law.*

**Proprietor:** Sable Windrunner (Halfling Innkeeper)

**Specialties:** General goods and supplies
