*A traveling merchant encountered during an encounter. This secretive half-orc guard born to merchants or artisans. Their parents were entertainers. In their youth, they grew up during wartime. They knew an athletic or competitive person who greatly influenced them. At one point, they lied to further their goals, motivated by money. Now they are escorting goods to market.*

**Proprietor:** Rolf Blackstone (Half-Orc Guard)

**Specialties:** General goods and supplies
